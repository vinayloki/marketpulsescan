ℹ️ About & User Guide

Everything you need to know about this scanner

🎯 What is SwingScan India?

SwingScan India is a free, automated stock scanner that tracks the performance of 2,200+ NSE-listed stocks across 6 timeframes (1 week to 12 months). It identifies the strongest momentum movers and enriches them with fundamental data like P/E, Market Cap, Sector, and 52-week range.

Built for swing traders and investors who want a quick, data-driven view of which NSE stocks are moving — and whether they're backed by solid fundamentals.

⚙️ How It Works

  • 1. Every weekday at 4:45 PM IST, an automated bot fetches all NSE tickers from the official NSE API.
  • 2. It downloads 13 months of daily closing prices from Yahoo Finance for every stock.
  • 3. It calculates % change across 6 timeframes: 1W, 2W, 1M, 3M, 6M, 12M.
  • 4. For the top ~85 movers, it fetches fundamental data (P/E, Market Cap, Sector, EPS, etc.) in parallel.
  • 5. It scrapes the latest market news from LiveMint and Economic Times RSS feeds.
  • 6. All data is pushed to this website automatically — no manual intervention.

📖 How to Read the Data

  • % Change columns (1W–12M): Shows how much a stock's price moved over that period. Formula: ((Today - Past) / Past) × 100
  • Green ▲ = price went UP · Red ▼ = price went DOWN
  • Bold numbers = strong moves (>10% medium bold, >50% extra bold)
  • P/E Ratio: Price-to-Earnings — lower generally means "cheaper" relative to earnings
  • Market Cap: Total company value (in ₹ Crores)
  • EPS: Earnings Per Share (TTM) — how much the company earned per share
  • 52W Range: The stock's lowest and highest price in the last 52 weeks
  • Dividend Yield: Annual dividend as a % of stock price
  • Book Value: Net asset value per share on the balance sheet

🏷️ Auto-Tag Legend

Stocks on the Top Movers tab are automatically tagged based on their data:

  • Value P/E below 15 — Stock is priced cheaply relative to earnings. May indicate undervaluation.
  • High P/E P/E above 50 — Expensive relative to earnings. Could signal overvaluation or growth premium.
  • Income Dividend Yield above 3% — Pays meaningful dividends. Attractive for income-seeking investors.
  • Multibagger 12-month return above 100% — Stock has more than doubled in the last year.
  • Breakout 1-week return above 20% — Sharp recent surge. Could indicate a breakout or momentum event.

🚫 Limitations

  • No intraday data: Scanner uses daily closing prices only — not live/real-time quotes.
  • ~80 fundamentals only: Fundamental data (P/E, Sector, etc.) is fetched for the top ~85 movers, not all 2,200 stocks.
  • No buy/sell signals: This is a momentum + fundamentals scanner, not a trading algorithm. It shows data, not recommendations.
  • Yahoo Finance data: Occasionally incomplete for small/micro-cap stocks. Some fields may show "—".
  • No BSE stocks: Only NSE-listed equities are scanned.
