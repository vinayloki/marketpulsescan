🎯 What is MarketPulse India?

A free, fully-automated NSE stock intelligence dashboard that tracks 2,200+ NSE stocks across 6 timeframes, identifies high-conviction trade setups, forecasts next-week direction, and shows which sectors lead or lag — updated every weekday at 4:00 PM IST.

  • ✅ 2,200+ NSE stocks scanned daily
  • ✅ 6-timeframe performance (1W → 12M)
  • ✅ Scored trade setups (Breakout · Volume · EMA)
  • ✅ Next-week BUY/SELL/HOLD forecast + SL + Target
  • ✅ Stock states: SQUEEZE · QUIET · LEADER
  • ✅ Daily sector rotation heatmap (last 10 days)
  • ✅ 15 canonical sector labels across all data

⚙️ How It Works — The Full Pipeline

  • 4:00 PM IST: GitHub Actions triggers the scanner (Mon–Fri)
  • Step 1: All 2,200+ NSE tickers fetched from NSE API
  • Step 2: 13 months OHLCV downloaded from Yahoo Finance for every stock
  • Step 3: % returns computed: 1W, 2W, 1M, 3M, 6M, 12M
  • Step 4: Fundamentals fetched for top ~85 movers (P/E, MCap, Sector, EPS)
  • Step 5: Sector labels normalized to 15 canonical India-standard categories
  • Step 6: Trade Setup engine scores every stock (52W breakout, volume, EMA)
  • Step 7: Prediction engine generates BUY/SELL/HOLD + confidence + SL/TP
  • Step 8: Stock state classified: SQUEEZE / QUIET / LEADER via ATR, BB, RSI, EMA
  • Step 9: Sector heatmap computed: equal-weighted avg return per sector per day
  • Step 10: All JSON files committed → GitHub Pages serves them statically
  • 📅 Data typically live by ~4:15 PM IST. Hard-refresh (Ctrl+Shift+R) after that.

📜 Stock States: SQUEEZE · QUIET · LEADER

Every stock in the Next-Week Forecast tab is classified into one of 4 states. Filter chips let you show only stocks in a specific state.

StateDefinitionTrading Action
📜 SQUEEZE Bollinger Band width < 20th percentile AND ATR is low. Stock is coiling — a directional breakout is building. Wait for breakout above upper BB on strong volume. Enter on the breakout candle; stop below recent swing low.
📉 QUIET ATR < 1.5% of price AND volume below 70% of 20-day avg. Low-activity phase — possible accumulation or distribution. Watchlist only. Enter when volume expands to 2×+ average — that's the wakeup signal.
🏆 LEADER 1M return > Nifty 50 AND RSI(14) > 55 AND price above 50-EMA. Confirmed relative strength — outperforming the index. Highest priority. Buy on pullbacks to 20-EMA. Institutions are actively accumulating these.
— NEUTRAL No strong signal in either direction. Normal trading range. Lower priority. Screen after LEADER and SQUEEZE.

💡 Bull market: Focus on LEADER stocks — proven outperformers. Sideways market: Focus on SQUEEZE — the next directional move candidates.

🎯 Trade Setups — Score & Signal Tags

Cards that passed multiple technical filters. Score 0–100 = how many signals align.

  • Score 75+: 🔥 High conviction — multiple strong signals aligned. Act on these first.
  • Score 50–74: Solid setup. Validate on TradingView before entry.
  • Score <50: Weak — only 1–2 signals. Lower priority.

Signal tags:

  • 📈 52W BREAKOUT: Closed above 52-week high — classic institutional momentum signal.
  • 📊 VOLUME SPIKE: Volume ≥ 2× 20-day average — strong participation confirms the move.
  • 🚀 EMA MOMENTUM: 20-EMA crossed above 50-EMA — medium-term trend just turned bullish.
  • 📊 HIGH VOLUME: Volume meaningfully above average — active buyer interest.

💡 Check the Sector Rank Strip first. If the card's sector is green in the heatmap, you have sector tailwind + stock momentum — strongest possible combination.

🚫 Limitations — What This Is Not

  • No intraday data: End-of-day closing prices only — not live quotes.
  • ~85 fundamentals only: P/E, Sector etc. for the top ~85 movers. Most small-caps show “—”.
  • Sector heatmap coverage: Based on ~85 stocks with known fundamentals — indicative, not market-wide.
  • ATR proxy for SQUEEZE: Uses ATR-based proxy (not full ADX). Validate on a live chart before acting.
  • Predictions are probabilistic: Check Accuracy Dashboard. Use signals most confidently when precision >55%.
  • Yahoo Finance data: Occasionally incomplete for small/micro-cap stocks.
  • NSE equities only: No BSE-only stocks, no F&O, no ETFs, no indices.
